ABOUT

I am a graduate student at the University of Washington in Computer Science & Engineering.

I work with Tadayoshi Kohno in the Security & Privacy Research Lab. I'm broadly interested in security and privacy, but I am particularly drawn to projects that seek to understand users' security and privacy expectations and how those expectations may be violated even when systems work as anticipated (e.g., are not compromised by malicious actors).

More information about my relevant skills and experiences is available in my CV.
